Thank you for your interest in contributing to NovaDream!

4. Your contribution is your own original work and you have the right to submit it.

## How to Contribute

- Fork the repository and create a branch for your change
- Make your changes and test them
- Open a pull request with a clear description of what you changed and why
- For bug reports, open an issue with as much detail as possible including your distro, compositor, and store affected
